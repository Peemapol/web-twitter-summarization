About Us

Background

This project was initiated upon observing the increasing advancement in Natural Language Processing technology, particularly the notable projects of ChatGPT by OpenAI and Bard by Google, which have served as sources of inspiration for us in creating a machine learning model that can achieve similar feats. However, due to constraints on time and our vested interest in social media, we have narrowed down the project scope to the summarization of hashtags sourced from Twitter.

User Problem

We conducted user testing to evaluate the viability of our AI-based solution for assisting Twitter users, and the results revealed certain issues that our product can address. These include spam tweets, dissemination of false information, and the presence of an overwhelming amount of contextual information within a single hashtag. Our product is designed to display only the key ideas of a given topic, thereby enabling us to overcome these challenges.
